# McDonald's-Financial-Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to explore customer segmentation, shopping behavior, and corporate financial performance trends from 2002–2022.


## Purpose  
The Tableau Insights Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer behavior in retail and e‑commerce, alongside financial performance analysis of McDonald’s. It helps analysts uncover market segments, predict customer preferences, and evaluate corporate KPIs over time.  

This tool is intended for use by business analysts, marketing teams, and financial strategists to optimize decision-making and identify growth opportunities.


## Tech Stack   
- 📊 **Tableau Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report creation  
- 📂 **Data Connections** – CSV files and structured datasets integrated into Tableau  
- 🧠 **Calculated Fields** – Used for KPIs, ratios, and trend analysis  
- 📝 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established among customer, purchase, and financial tables for cross-filtering  
- 📁 **File Format** – `.twbx` for development and `.png` for dashboard previews  

## Data Source  
- *E‑Commerce Insights Document* – Customer segmentation, profiling, shopping behavior, and channel preferences  
- *McDonald’s Financial Statements (CSV)* – Historical financial data from 2002–2022 including revenue, earnings, margins, debt, and market capitalization  

Data includes:  
- Customer demographics, purchase categories, device preferences, payment methods, and shipping choices  
- KPIs such as average purchase amount, return rate, loyalty program membership, and satisfaction levels  
- McDonald’s corporate financial metrics: revenue, earnings, EPS, operating margin, debt levels, and dividend yield  


##  Features   

### • Business Problem  
Retailers and corporations generate massive amounts of customer and financial data, yet decision-makers often lack an intuitive way to segment customers, predict behavior, and track financial health.  

Key questions such as:  
- Which demographics drive the highest purchase activity?  
- How do payment and shipping preferences vary by channel?  
- What are McDonald’s long-term revenue and margin trends?  
… are difficult to answer quickly with raw data.  

### • Goal of the Dashboard  
To deliver an interactive visual tool that:  
- Enables segmentation of customers by demographics, preferences, and behavior  
- Supports predictive analytics for spending habits and loyalty  
- Provides financial insights into McDonald’s performance over time  
- Helps optimize marketing, sales, and corporate strategy  

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals  
- 👥 **Market Segmentation**: Popular purchase categories by gender, age vs. income, and location activity  
- 🧑‍💻 **Customer Profiling**: Purchase intent by education level, device preferences, and discount sensitivity  
- 🛒 **Shopping Behavior Trends**: Impact of decision-making time on purchase amount, age vs. intent analysis  
- 💳 **Channel & Preferences**: Most used payment methods and shipping preferences by channel  
- 📈 **Financial KPIs**: Revenue growth (peak in 2011–2013 at $28B), earnings performance, assets vs. liabilities, and market valuation ratios (P/E, P/B)  
- 📊 **Profitability Analysis**: Operating margin vs. EPS correlation across years  
- 📌 **KPI Summary**: Average purchase amount, return rate, loyalty program membership %, and purchase trends over time  

### • Business Impact & Insights  
- 🎯 **Marketing Optimization**: Identify key customer segments and tailor campaigns by demographics and preferences  
- 🔮 **Predictive Strategy**: Forecast spending habits and loyalty to improve retention  
- 🛍️ **Retail Efficiency**: Optimize payment and shipping channels for smoother customer experience  
- 💼 **Financial Analysis**: Track McDonald’s corporate health, profitability, and shareholder value over two decades  
- 🌍 **Strategic Expansion**: Spot underperforming regions or demographics with untapped potential
